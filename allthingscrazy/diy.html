The best sites for DIY home decorating.

HGTV WEBSITE: https://www.hgtv.com This site shows you the latest trends, simple DIYs and photo inspirations. Plus, you can check out some of the best design shows. This is my favorite binge-watching channel.

BRIT+CO:https://www.brit.co/living/home-diy/ BRIT+CO is a great site for home DIYs. This site is so fun, it gives many colorful DIYs to add to your home. You can find alternatives to those expensive décor pieces and even a way to add an amazing backsplash with peel & stick!

MARTHASTEWART: https://www.marthastewart.com/1505907/home-design There is something for everyone on the Martha Stewart website. This site not only has lots of décor DIYs but it also offers tons of inspiration show-casing beautiful homes.

GOODHOUSEKEEPING: https://www.goodhousekeeping.com Goodhousekeeping is right up there with HGTV, showing you how to put together some great designs on a budget with a lot of DIYs. Goodhousekeeping is one of the best magazines subscriptions you can have to get inspiration such as seasonal decor, interiors, and handmade goods.
