The best sites for DIY home decorating.

HGTV: https://www.hgtv.com/ This site shows you the latest trends, simple DIYs and photo inspirations. Plus, you can check out some of the best design shows. This is my favorite binge-watching channel.

BRIT+CO: https://www.brit.co/living/home-diy/ BRIT+CO is a great site for home DIYs. This site is so fun, it gives many colorful DIYs to add to your home. You can find alternatives to those expensive décor pieces and even a way to add an amazing backsplash with peel & stick!

Martha Stewart: https://www.marthastewart.com/1505907/home-design There is something for everyone on the Martha Stewart website. This site not only has lots of décor DIYs but it also offers tons of inspiration show-casing beautiful homes.
